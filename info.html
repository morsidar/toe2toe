History of TOE2TOE

Our socks are roughly divided into five categories: occupation of the wearer, color, size, odor and bodily fluid. When interested in an order, you will be able to freely choose one of the categories. The rest, however, remain a secret until you receive your order.

Example:

If you choose the occupation of the wearer to be “teacher”, this is the only characteristic of the sock that you will know.

After receiving your order, someone from our team will pick out a sock to match your requirements and package your order.

Basis prize for a sock is 50€. If you buy more than one sock, the basis price drops by 10€ for each one. If you wish to choose more than one category, you will have to pay 25€ extra for each. Free shipping in all of Europe except the UK.

An order includes: Your sock(s) professionally reviewed and packaged by us; a certificate of authenticity.

That's a little history about TOE2TOE.
